How It Works

From Brief to
Final Delivery

A clear, structured process designed to keep your project on time and your team informed at every stage.

01
Step One
Submit Your Quote Request
Tell us about your project using our quote request form. Describe the scope, select the rendering types you need, and upload whatever reference materials you have — architectural drawings, FF&E specs, inspiration imagery, or anything that helps communicate your vision.
Don't worry if your drawing set isn't complete. We work with projects at every stage of design and will identify exactly what we need during scoping. The more you share, the more accurate our output — but we can always start a conversation first.
24hr quote turnaround
02
Step Two
Review & Approve Your Quote
Within 24 hours, we'll send you a detailed quote covering scope, pricing, deliverables, and timeline. This is the point to ask questions, clarify scope, or adjust the brief before anything begins.
Once you approve the quote, your client portal account is created and your project is officially in the queue. A 50% deposit is collected to begin production on floor plan orders; rendering project payment terms are outlined in your quote.
No payment until you approve
03
Step Three
Production Begins
Your project moves into production. Our team models, lights, and renders your project to spec. You'll be able to track the stage of your project in real time through your client portal — from pre-production through to first draft.
For larger or more complex projects, we may reach out during production with clarifying questions. We'd rather pause and confirm than deliver something off-spec.
4–8 weeks standard · 2–4 weeks expedited
04
Step Four
Draft Review via ReviewStudio
When your first draft is ready, you'll receive a notification in your client portal. A ReviewStudio link lets you view, annotate, and leave pinned comments directly on the renderings — no email threads, no confusion about which image you mean.
All projects include 2 full draft rounds plus a final round. For each round, review the draft, leave your comments, then click "Confirm Review Complete" in your portal when done. This signals our team to begin the next round of revisions immediately.
2 rounds of revision + final round
05
Step Five
Final Delivery
Once the final round is approved, your files are delivered directly to your client portal. High-resolution stills, web-optimised versions, 360° embed links, or video files — everything is organised and ready to download at any time.
Your files remain accessible in your portal indefinitely. All deliverables are 100% license-free — use them anywhere, for anything, without restriction.
100% license-free · yours to keep
